Overview

A cup of coffee is a dating app. The semi-serious relationship is a purpose of this app as not having a profile picture of users. The prominent feature is to certify users identity through a company email so that it makes a mood to make a serious relationship, although there is no profile picture.

As a lead designer and co-founder of the team, I came up with key features, conducted design sprint, created UX/UI design, built a branding, and planning promotions for marketing.

I researched around me and I found that the similar cases happen all the time. I had two complaints about dating apps on the market.

Observation
other dating apps

People too easily rate others by swiping profile pictures for one or two second no matter who they are. Many apps show users’ profile and intend to match them based on the appearance.

Key idea to solve
the problem

First and foremost, I found an identity certification. In some apps, people claim they are a doctor or working for Samsung, but it hasn't been verified it or they should send a picture of the business card to verify it.

I came up with what if user verify it by a company mail. Recently, the anonymous community app in Korea to verify their identity by it has been a huge success. I was inspired by the app. Simple and easy to certified through a company email and it provided 100 % reliability.

Find user goal
with Design Sprint

My team has come up with the ultimate user goal through the design sprint. Finally, we discussed what value we should provide users, based on the core concept of the app that users can verify their identity by a company email. 

The user goal of the most dating apps is to meet ‘A nice person’, but the standard of ‘A nice person’ is different from each dating app. Who is the common standard of ‘A nice person’ with limited information for people who want to go into a serious relationship by dating app?

01

The person who has specified profile information, so that users can get to know them as much as possible. There is no way to know the other side, but that they provide sufficient information. An insufficient information means that it gives the user insecurity. That’s why I provided the objective item and some scripts in order to users to easily write their information.

02

The person working for a reputable company. It is about a cultural background of Korean. It is the most effective information between strangers. As the reputation of a company represents the personal reputation, so that most expect that the person working for a nice company means a nice person, and they have a financial capability. Based on this case, we have targeted the users working for a decent company.

03

The person who constantly accesses the app. User’s latest active time is quite crucial, which shows whether they are a fake user or not. It makes it possible for users to expect that the other would accepts their dating offer when they send a request. It is one of the approaches to give them a confidence.

04

The person with good manner. The well-mannered in terms of dating app means that the other behave polite in chatting and respond in a quick. If the chatting goes wrong, the relationship will not move forward, and it will leave a poor user experience of the app. It matches with users who visit the app within four days. What’s more, It provides users with some tips to help chatting and talking about various subjects, so that they can be a nice conversation partner.

User Flow

When the product was launched, I created it with flow to pay twice that is similar to some dating apps in the Korean market. However, after the launching, I got some feedbacks that it is complexed and expensive. That’s why I redesigned the flow concise which made it possible for users feel simple and cheap.
